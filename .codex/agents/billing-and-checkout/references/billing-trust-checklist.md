# Billing Trust Checklist

## Clarity
- Is the selected plan obvious?
- Is the price obvious?
- Is the billing interval obvious?
- Is it clear what happens after clicking the CTA?

## Trust
- Does the UI feel calm and credible?
- Are important billing details visible at the moment of decision?
- Is the user protected from uncertainty?

## Management
- Is current plan/account state visible?
- Can the user understand how to update or cancel?
- Are failed payment states handled clearly?
