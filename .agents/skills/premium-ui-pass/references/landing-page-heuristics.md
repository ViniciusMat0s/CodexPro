# Landing Page Heuristics

## Above the fold
- Is the value proposition clear quickly?
- Is the primary CTA obvious?

## Flow
- Does the page move naturally from awareness to trust to action?
- Are sections ordered intentionally?

## Visual quality
- Does the page feel premium, not generic?
- Is spacing and typography strong?

## Conversion support
- Are trust signals clear?
- Is friction low?
- Is CTA repetition tasteful and strategic?

## Noise control
- Is there too much decorative clutter?
- Are there too many competing messages?
