# Form UX Heuristics

## Trust
- Does the form feel trustworthy and calm?
- Is the visual structure clear?

## Completion ease
- Is the form easy to scan and fill?
- Are related fields grouped logically?

## Validation
- Are errors understandable?
- Are required fields clear?
- Are submission states handled properly?

## User confidence
- Does the user know what happens on submit?
- Is success/failure feedback clear?

## Accessibility
- Labels are present
- Focus states are visible
- Inputs/buttons are semantically correct
